## üìù Notes

### Strike Price Mapping:
- **Bullish Call**: 100% (ATM), 110%, 120%, 130%
- **Bearish Put**: 100% (ATM), 90%, 80%, 70%
- **Strap/Strip**: Only 100% ATM available
- **Spreads**: Use spread percentage or strike price
- **High Volatility**: Straddle (110%), Strangle (115%, 120%, 130%)
- **Low Volatility**: Long Butterfly (115%, 125%), Long Condor (120%, 130%)

### Spread Strategies:
- **Bull Call Spread**: 110%, 120%, 130% (higher strikes)
- **Bull Put Spread**: 97%, 90% (lower strikes, inverse strategy)
- **Bear Put Spread**: 90%, 80%, 70% (lower strikes)
- **Bear Call Spread**: 103%, 110% (higher strikes, inverse strategy)
- **Straddle**: 110% (ATM, high volatility)
- **Strangle**: 115%, 120%, 130% (high volatility, wider spreads)
- **Long Butterfly**: 115%, 125% (low volatility, narrow range)
- **Long Condor**: 120%, 130% (low volatility, wider range)

### Contract Details:
- All contracts deployed on **Arbitrum Sepolia Testnet**
- **OperationalTreasury**: `0xdE19737db2325DD2aA072713aEaa7db4F4b23009`
- **USDC**: `0x75faf114eafb1BDbe2F0316DF893fd58CE46AA4d`
- Period configuration: 1 hour - 1 day
